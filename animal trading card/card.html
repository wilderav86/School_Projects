Ronin Toad

If eaten by a predator, toxic glands located behind a ronin toad's head will poison its attacker, causing terrifying hallucinations.

  • Scientific Name: Bufonidae Battleus
  • Average Length: 5 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 120 years
  • Habitat: Feudal Japan

Toothless and generally warty in appearance. The ronin toad tends to wander from lillypad to lillypad, selling their skills as warriors to any who require them.
